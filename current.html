About this course

This course will explore the current state of affairs in the education realm. We will analyze three areas: the constants, novelties, and cycles. During the first few weeks of the quarter, we will look at the constants - when these factors change, we notice. Students will grasp changes such as the general decrease in overall student enrollment in US degree-granting institutions as well as students’ adoption of a “checklist mentality” as they may robotically complete general education requirements (demonstrated in a case study from the University of Notre Dame). These changes among others emphasize the increasing need for another form of learning … one that happens online and in distance. Our class will also focus on the novelties or new factors/trends that have emerged. Major novelties include the rise in distance education, the textbook demise, and the surge of Massive Open Online Courses (or MOOC). Finally, this course will take a deep dive into the cycles that exist, specifically the need for a new type of education or a “learning revolution” as claimed by Sir Ken Robinson. Overall, students will increase their understanding of the past, current, and potential future state of affairs influencing the rise of online education.


In order to draw predictions regarding the future of online technology, note the novelties. The novelties encompass newfound ideas and important trends. For example, the decreasing amount of students studying on campus at higher education institutions in the US from 2012 to 2016 as well as the emergence of MOOCs provide a reason for the increasing number of students in distance education. Additionally, remember this equation: decline in student enrollment in higher education + increase in distance education among students = 6.4% decline in on-campus students in the US from 2012 - 2016 (data source). Will this trend continue? What other novelties are woven together to influence the future of education? What factors occurring in the future will affect this novelty? This course will explore the answers to these questions and more!
